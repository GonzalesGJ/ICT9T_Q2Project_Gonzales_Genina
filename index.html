Magellan's Cross

- This cross is a must-see momunument, as it is one of the most iconic historical landmark in Cebu City. Magellan's Cross is a reminder of how Christianity was brought in the Philippines.

Ariel's Point

- In Boracay, it is known for its 15 meter high "diving boards" where adventure junkies can leap off to the clear water. Even if you're not up for the thrill, this area has one of the clearest waters and richest marine life in Boracay.

Chocolate Hills

- These hills are the Philippines' third National Geographical Monuement, located on the island of Bohol. They also look like chocolate truffles due to their rounded hills appearance that go brown during the dry season.
